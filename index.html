Francesco Sarno

Computer Vision Research Engineer

I'm a computer vision and robotics research engineer working at Switzerland Innovation Park Biel/Bienne in the Swiss Battery Technology Center.

I received my Master's degree in Robotics, Systems and Control from ETH Zurich in 2021, and my Bachelor's degree in Automation Engineering from the Politecnico di Milano in 2018.

My research interests are computer vision, 3D vision, AutoML, deep learning and reinforcement learning.
